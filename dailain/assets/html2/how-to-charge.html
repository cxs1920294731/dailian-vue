HOW TO CHARGE
CHARGE YOUR BLUETOOTH ADAPTER

Please keep the Bluetooth transmitter charged by an 5V external charger such as Android power charger (instead of the USB jack ofTV) via Micro USB Power Cord for 2~3 hours. We recommend charging for 4 hours before using for the first time.

Model Working Time Per Full Charge
TT-BA01 Approximately 7 hours
TT-BA06 Approximately 10 hours
TT-BA07 Approximately 10hours
TT-BA08 Approximately 20 hours
TT-BA09 15 hours (AUX mode) / 12 hours (SPDIF mode)
