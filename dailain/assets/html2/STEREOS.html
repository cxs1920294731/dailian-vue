PAIR WITH TWO BLUETOOTH STEREOS
PAIR WITH TWO BLUETOOTH STEREOS AT THE SAME TIME TT-BA07/TT-BA08/TT-BA09

NOTICE:TT-BA01/TT-BA06 can NOT pair with two Bluetooth stereos at the same time.
1. Follow the instructions in the "Pairing" section in user manual to pair the transmitter with your 1st Bluetooth stereo.
2. Turn off the Bluetooth function on your 1st device, tap the Power Button twice to pair and connect to your 2nd stereo.
3. Turn on the Bluetooth function on your 1st Bluetooth stereo. In approximately 30 seconds, the 2nd stereo will automatically connect in.
4. Both Bluetooth stereos should receive audio successfully now.
